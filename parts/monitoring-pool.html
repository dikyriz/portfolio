Monitoring dan Kontrol Kolam Renang dengan Android

Create a swimming pool monitoring system to determine water quality and control the water pumps.
This system is used to monitor water quality, starting from its turbidity and pH.
The numbers obtained from the sensor will be displayed on the Android application, with a display that is easy for the user to read.
Apart from that, the application has buttons for draining and filling water through a pump that can be controlled through the application.

Stack

  • Arduino / C
  • Kotlin
